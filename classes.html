Our Curriculum | 我们的课程

We offer Chinese language classes for all year levels, from Foundation through to Year 12. Our curriculum is aligned with the Victorian Curriculum and Assessment Authority (VCAA) framework, ensuring students receive education that meets Victorian standards.

本校为预备年级至12年级的所有学生提供中文课程，课程内容与维多利亚课程及评估局（VCAA）框架紧密对接，确保学生接受符合维多利亚州标准的优质教育。

Small Class Sizes | 小班教学

Class sizes are intentionally kept small, allowing our teachers to give every student the individual attention they deserve. Our native-speaking teachers bring both professional expertise and cultural authenticity to every lesson.

本校坚持小班教学，让每位教师能够充分关注每位学生的学习进度与需求。我们的母语教师将专业教学经验与中华文化底蕴融入每一堂课。

Cultural Activities | 文化活动

Students participate in cultural activities and events throughout the year, including Lunar New Year, Mid-Autumn Festival, and other significant celebrations.

学生全年参与各类文化活动，包括农历新年、中秋节等重要节日的庆祝活动，在欢乐中感受中华文化的魅力。
