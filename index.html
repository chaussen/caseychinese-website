About

A school built by families.

关于我们

Casey Foundation Chinese School is a parent-founded, volunteer-run weekend school serving the Casey community. We offer approachable, joyful Mandarin instruction grounded in Chinese culture.

We believe language is a doorway — to family, to heritage, and to the world.

Every Saturday, classrooms fill with children learning to read, write, sing, and speak Mandarin. Our small class sizes mean each student is known by name, and our curriculum balances structured literacy with cultural arts: calligraphy, paper-cutting, music, dance, and seasonal celebrations.

我们相信，语言是通往家庭、传承与世界的桥梁。每周六的课堂，孩子们在小班教学中学习汉语读写， 同时体验书法、剪纸、音乐、舞蹈与传统节日，让语言学习自然而温暖。

Programs

Three pathways, one community.

课程设置

Whether your child is starting their first words or refining their essays, we have a level that meets them where they are. Placement is friendly and flexible.

01

Beginning Level

初级班 · Prep – Grade 3

An introduction to Mandarin through songs, stories, and guided play. Students build foundational tones, vocabulary, and a love of the language from their very first lesson.

Prep – Gr 3 2:15 – 5:00pm
02

Intermediate Level

中级班 · Grades 4 – 8

Building reading, writing, and spoken fluency through structured literacy, character recognition, and conversational practice paired with cultural workshops each term.

Gr 4 – 8 2:15 – 5:00pm
03

Advanced Level

高级班 · Grades 9 – VCE

Advanced reading, essay writing, and conversation for older students. Builds the skills and confidence needed for VCE Chinese and lifelong language fluency.

Gr 9 – VCE 2:15 – 5:00pm
