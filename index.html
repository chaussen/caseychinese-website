About

A school built by families.

关于我们

Casey Foundation Chinese School is a parent-founded, volunteer-run school at the heart of the Casey community. Every Saturday we bring children and families together through warm, engaging Mandarin classes grounded in Chinese culture. Casey中文学校扎根凯西社区，每周六为孩子们带来温暖、活泼的中文课堂，将语言教学与中华文化有机融合。

We believe language is a doorway — to family, to heritage, and to the world.

Every Saturday, classrooms fill with children learning to read, write, sing, and speak Mandarin. Our small class sizes mean each student is known by name, and our curriculum balances structured literacy with cultural arts: calligraphy, paper-cutting, music, dance, and seasonal celebrations.

我们相信，语言是连接家庭、传承与世界的桥梁。每周六，孩子们在小班教学中学习汉语读写， 同时通过书法、剪纸、音乐、舞蹈与传统节日庆典，让语言学习充满生命力。

Programs

Three pathways, one community.

课程设置

Whether your child is starting their first words or refining their essays, we have a level that meets them where they are. Placement is friendly and flexible. 无论您的孩子是初学中文还是已有基础，我们都有合适的班级。分班灵活亲切，欢迎咨询。

01

Beginning Level

初级班 · Prep – Grade 3

An introduction to Mandarin through songs, stories, and guided play. Students build foundational tones, vocabulary, and a love of the language from their very first lesson. 通过歌谣、故事与游戏，轻松迈入中文世界，培养语感与学习兴趣。

Prep – Gr 3 Saturdays
02

Intermediate Level

中级班 · Grades 4 – 8

Building reading, writing, and spoken fluency through structured literacy, character recognition, and conversational practice paired with cultural workshops each term. 系统学习汉字读写与日常会话，每学期结合文化主题活动，全面提升语言能力。

Gr 4 – 8 Saturdays
03

Advanced Level

高级班 · Grades 9 – VCE

Advanced reading, essay writing, and conversation for older students. Builds the skills and confidence needed for VCE Chinese and lifelong language fluency. 深化阅读、写作与口语，为VCE中文及终身流利表达打好基础。

Gr 9 – VCE Saturdays

Teach with us.

加入我们

We are always looking for dedicated volunteers to join our teaching team. Teaching experience or a background in Chinese language, education, or a related field is valued. Most of all, we look for a genuine love of Mandarin and working with children. 我们诚邀有志于中文教育的人士加入志愿教学团队。具备教学经验，或拥有中文、教育及相关领域背景者尤为欢迎。我们最看重的，是对中文教育的热忱与对孩子的真诚关爱。
