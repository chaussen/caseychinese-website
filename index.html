About

A school built by families.

关于我们

Our school is part of a proud tradition. Our main school has been committed to Chinese language education in Melbourne since 1971 — making it one of the longest-serving Chinese educational organisations in Victoria. That legacy of community, culture, and language learning is the foundation we stand on.

The Casey campus opened in 2017, bringing this tradition to the south-east. We have grown into a school the local community calls its own — small enough that every child is known by name, strong enough to carry a lineage that spans generations.

我们的主校1971年成立于墨尔本，数十年薪火相传，是维州华人社区最具历史的中文教育机构之一。语言与文化，是我们办学的根基。

凯西校区2017年落地东南区，文脉绵延，与社区共同生长。课堂虽小，师生相知；根深叶茂，生生不息。

Casey Foundation Chinese School is rooted in the Casey community. Every Saturday, children learn Mandarin in a warm classroom — language and culture coming together naturally.

Casey中文学校植根凯西社区，每周六，孩子们相聚课堂，语言为纽带，文化铭于心间。

We believe language is a doorway — to family, to heritage, and to the world. Our small class sizes mean each student is known by name, and our curriculum balances structured literacy with cultural arts: calligraphy, paper-cutting, music, dance, and seasonal celebrations.

语言是一扇门——通往家庭，通往血脉，通往世界。小班教学，有名有姓，有声有色。书法、剪纸、音乐、舞蹈，岁时节令，一一经历。

Programs

Three pathways, one community.

课程设置

Whether your child is starting their first words or refining their essays, we have a level that meets them where they are. Placement is based on your child's Chinese ability — not their school year. Students without any Chinese background will typically start in the Beginning level, and that is completely normal. We assess every new student individually during their free trial. 分班依据孩子的中文能力——而非学校年级。没有任何中文基础的学生，通常会从启蒙班开始，这完全正常。我们会在免费试课期间对每位新生进行个别评估。

01

Beginning Level

启蒙兴趣班 · Prep – Year 3

An introduction to Mandarin through songs, stories, and guided play. Students build foundational tones, vocabulary, and a love of the language from their very first lesson. 通过歌谣、故事与游戏，轻松迈入中文世界，培养语感与学习兴趣。

Prep – Yr 3 Saturdays
02

Intermediate Level

系统教材班 · Years 4 – 8

Building reading, writing, and spoken fluency through structured literacy, character recognition, and conversational practice paired with cultural workshops each term. 系统学习汉字读写与日常会话，每学期结合文化主题活动，全面提升语言能力。

Yr 4 – 8 Saturdays
03

Advanced Level

高级班 · Years 9 – VCE

Advanced reading, essay writing, and conversation for older students. Builds the skills and confidence needed for VCE Chinese and lifelong language fluency. 深化阅读、写作与口语，为VCE中文及终身流利表达打好基础。

Yr 9 – VCE Saturdays

Common Questions

Questions we hear often.

常见问题

Many families have similar questions when they first get to know us. Don't worry — we hope the answers below help. 很多家长刚开始认识我们，都会有类似的疑问。别担心，下面这些解答希望能帮到您

My child has never learned Chinese before. Which class is right for them?

我的孩子从未学过中文，适合上哪个班？

Students without any Chinese background start from the Beginning level (启蒙班), regardless of their school year. It is a relaxed, fun environment — the focus is on building confidence and a genuine love of the language. We confirm the right level during the free trial, and students can move between levels at any time. 没有中文基础的孩子，建议从启蒙班开始，先在轻松有趣的环境里培养语感和兴趣。我们会在免费试课期间确认最合适的班级，之后也可以随时根据孩子的进度调整。

I have two children at different levels. Can they both attend?

我有两个孩子，程度不同，他们都可以来上课吗？

Yes. Many of our families have children across multiple levels. Both children can attend on the same Saturday. 可以。我们许多家庭都有就读不同班级的孩子，两位孩子可以在同一个周六一起上课。

Is there homework or assessment?

有家庭作业或考试吗？

We give children an appropriate amount of weekly practice to help reinforce what was covered in class — enough to be useful, not so much that it becomes a burden. There is a summative assessment every two terms. There are no formal report cards — our teachers keep in touch with parents to share how each child is getting on. 每周会布置适量的练习，帮孩子把课堂所学记得更牢，分量适中不会造成压力。每两个学期会有一次阶段性评估，但不设正式成绩单——老师会随时跟家长沟通，分享孩子的学习情况。
