Welcome | 欢迎

Welcome to Casey Chinese School — proudly serving the Casey community since 1970. Every Saturday afternoon, we provide Chinese language and cultural education for students of all year levels in a warm, supportive environment. Small classes, experienced native-speaking teachers, and a strong sense of community make us the natural choice for families in Melbourne's southeast seeking quality Chinese education.

欢迎来到Casey中文学校——自1970年起，我们一直扎根凯西社区，为各年级学生提供中文语言与文化教育。每周六下午，在温馨友善的学习环境中，小班教学与经验丰富的母语教师相结合，让我们成为墨尔本东南区华人家庭的首选中文学校。
