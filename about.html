About Us | 关于我们

Our Story | 我们的故事

Casey Chinese School has been nurturing Chinese language and cultural heritage in the Casey community since 1970. Now an incorporated association under Victorian law, our school is committed to delivering quality Chinese education aligned with the Victorian Curriculum and Assessment Authority (VCAA).

Casey中文学校自1970年起，致力于在凯西社区传承中文语言与中华文化。本校现已在维多利亚州正式注册为社团法人，严格按照维多利亚课程与评估局（VCAA）的框架提供优质中文教育。

[Photo]

Our Mission | 我们的使命

We believe language is the foundation of cultural identity. Our experienced native-speaking teachers create a supportive learning environment where students develop strong language skills alongside a deep appreciation for Chinese culture and traditions.

我们相信，语言是文化认同的根基。本校经验丰富的母语教师营造了积极向上的学习氛围，让学生在掌握扎实语言能力的同时，深入了解中华文化与传统。

[Photo]

Culture & Community | 文化与社区

Throughout the year, we celebrate Chinese festivals and host cultural events that enrich our students' understanding of their heritage and bring our school community together.

每年，本校举办丰富多彩的中国节日庆典与文化活动，加深学生对中华文化的认识，凝聚学校社区的向心力。
