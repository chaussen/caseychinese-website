Ode to the Goose · 唐 · 骆宾王 Luò Bīnwáng

Goose, goose, goose — neck curved, singing to the sky. White feathers afloat on green water, red feet paddling the clear waves.

By tradition, 骆宾王 wrote this at seven years old — which is why it is so many children's very first poem. You already know 水 from the explorer above.
